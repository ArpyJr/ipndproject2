How To Make Spaghetti Aglio E Olio Out At Sea

Jooyong Chung 05.08.2017

Ingredient List

  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Italian Parsley
  • Spaghetti
  • Fresh Caught Prawns
  • Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • Mozzarella

Directions

  1. Compost organic matter
  2. Plant parsley seeds
  3. Steal flour and olives
  4. Make olive oil
  5. Make spaghetti
  6. Harvest parsley
  7. Put everything in a pan
  8. Cook for 10 minutes
